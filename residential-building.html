Decription

The residential building is situated in the central area of the city Varna at the intersection of str. “Sredna Gora” and bul. “Vl. Varnenchik” The ground level is occupied by one shop and a coffee bar. Car parking is assured in one underground level. The storage spaces for all flats are in the underground level. All the apartments are situated north-south and east. Designed in four types with one, two or three bedrooms and penthouses which have their own beautiful green terrace transform in small green yards. This is a conceptual idea to create a self-sustainable building, stripped away from the chaos of the busy city, while providing a “green” calmness and (security). At the same time, the building is situated in close proximity to essential utilitarian services. The project shows the interest towards the merging of the benefits of single and multi-family buildings with a higher standard of living while creating relaxing atmosphere and social surroundings. In search of a new (structure), the building has improved energy costs and provides comfort to its occupiers. The material used for the facades is white brick. The other materials wood and metal aluminum emphasize the feeling of comfort and coziness.
