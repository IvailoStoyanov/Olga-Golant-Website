Description

The sports center is located in the city Varna, region Asparuhovo. The situation is placed underneath Asparuhov Bridge, on the place of an already existing football field. On the right of the sports center there is a sewer connecting Cherno More with Lake Varna. The region is suitable for sport activities defined as such according to MP (Master Planning, known as OYP in Bulgaria), city Varna. The necessity of sport facilities not only in the city but in the whole country supports the building of more sport areas and buildings. The project aims to combine nature, new technologies, the opportunities for unique lighting and the shapes of an organic architecture. The whole idea is inspired by the fact that the building is situated so close to the water. One of the main sports that will be practiced there is swimming. From there comes the idea for the whirlpool based on the Fibonacci dependence. The silhouette of the building aims to elegantly blend in with its surroundings while creating the feeling of the slightly hilly region and a lush sea.
